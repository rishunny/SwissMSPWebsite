Welcome to the MSP Switzerland page.

We're a bunch of students passionate about technology from all over Switzerland. Our goal is to promote Microsoft products from HoloLens, Xbox, Kinect and Surface to services like Azure, Cognitive Services or Office across Switzerland. To achieve this goal we organize events such as hackathons and talks.
